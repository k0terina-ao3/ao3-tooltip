How do I add bold, italic or strikethrough text?
**text** — two asterisks on each side makes it bold.
*text* — one asterisk on each side makes it italic.
~~text~~ — two tildes makes it struck through.
Need a real asterisk or tilde that shouldn't turn into formatting? Put a backslash right before it: \* or \~
